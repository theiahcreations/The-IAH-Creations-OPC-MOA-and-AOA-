\noindent \textbf{III. The objects for which the Company is established are:} \\

\noindent \textbf{(A) THE MAIN OBJECTS TO BE PURSUED BY THE COMPANY ON ITS INCORPORATION ARE:}

\begin{enumerate}[label=\arabic*., leftmargin=*]
    \item \textbf{Software Development \& Digital Solutions:} To carry on the business of designing, developing, testing, deploying, and maintaining commercial-grade software solutions, including rapid digital prototyping, full-scale software development, and minimum viable products (MVPs) for global markets.

    \item \textbf{Website Design \& Development:} To engage in the business of creating professional digital presence solutions, including Single Page Websites (SPW) for portfolios and resumes, and Dynamic Multi-Page Websites for corporate businesses, e-commerce platforms, and agencies, utilizing Content Management Systems (CMS) and payment gateway integrations.

    \item \textbf{Web Application Services:} To develop, maintain, and host browser-based software and Single Page Applications (SPA) using modern frameworks (React, Angular, Vue, etc.) for utilities such as calculators, dashboards, and to-do lists; and to create Multi-Tasking Dynamic Applications for enterprise needs, including SaaS products, Customer Relationship Management (CRM) systems, HR portals, and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solutions.

    \item \textbf{Mobile Application Development:} To design, develop, publish, and maintain Native and Hybrid mobile applications for Android and iOS platforms, ranging from lightweight utility apps and web-view wrappers to full-featured dynamic applications involving user authentication, real-time databases, GPS integration, and AI-powered features.

    \item \textbf{Hosting, Automation \& Infrastructure:} To provide comprehensive hosting solutions, cloud integration, premium CDN services, and business workflow automation, acting on the company motto "Innovate, Automate, Host," and to facilitate server management, domain services, and third-party API integrations.

    \item \textbf{Advanced Technology Integration:} To research, develop, and integrate advanced technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), microservices architecture, and real-time data analytics into business software to ensure scalability and future-readiness for clients.
\end{enumerate}
